Automatic watering system
×

Authors:

Platons K

Artyom D

(Grade 10)

Irrigation can be a big challenge for farmers and gardeners. Manual watering takes a lot of time, up to 10 hours a week for just one acre, and wastes a lot of water. In fact, up to 60% of water used in traditional irrigation is lost to evaporation or runoff. With water becoming scarce, especially in farming where it uses 70% of all freshwater, this is a serious issue.
